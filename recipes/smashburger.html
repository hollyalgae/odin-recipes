Smash Burger

Description

This burger gonna suck you off wet and sloppy.

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger guns
  • 2 tablespoons butter, soft
  • 1 pound ground chuck beef
  • 4 6-inch squares parchment paper
  • salt
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • burger toppings of choice

Steps

It goes like this. Preheat your outdoor grill to heat and oil its grate. Get a cast iron and put it on there too

Butter those buns and slap em on there to get them browned

Make the meat into eight different 2 ounce balls, don't do it too tight or you'll fuck it up. Place em on there, cover em with parchment paper so nothing sticks, smash that shit.

salt it or it'll taste bad. grill for 45 seconds and then flip them and put cheese 4 of the patties. give it another 20 seconds and then stack the cheeseless patties on the cheese patties. put it on a bun and add toppings.

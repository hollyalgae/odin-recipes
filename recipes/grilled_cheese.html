Description

gwilled cheese :D

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Kraft cheese
  • Butter

Steps

Heat a skillet to medium. Spread mayo or butter on 2 slices of Bread Put one slice of bread spread-side down onto the pan Add however many slices of cheese you desire Put the other slice of bread with the spread facing away from the cheese Check occassionally and once one side is toasted as desired, flip and toast until done as desired.
